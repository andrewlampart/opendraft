## Issues Identified

### CRITICAL (Must Fix)

### MODERATE (Should Fix)

**Issue 1: Potential "Geography" Promise vs. Delivery**
- **Location:** Introduction (Para 2) vs. Results (2.3)
- **Problem:** The Introduction heavily emphasizes the unique geography of Pomerania ("specyfika geograficzna... dostęp do Morza Bałtyckiego... ruch turystyczny") as a key driver for the study. However, the Results snippet focuses almost exclusively on *economic efficiency* (10 mln PLN savings) and *staffing models* (doctors vs. paramedics).
- **Fix:** Ensure Section 2.3 or 2.4 explicitly connects the "Telemedicine/Paramedic model" back to the *specific* challenges of the Baltic coast/tourism. For example: "How does the paramedic-only model handle the summer tourist spike?" If this is missing, the Intro writes a check the Results don't cash.

### MINOR (Nice to Fix)

**Issue 2: Terminology Overload regarding "Model"**
- **Locations:** Sections 2.1, 2.3, and Appendix A
- **Problem:** The word "Model" is used for three different concepts:
 1. Organizational systems (French-German model vs. Anglo-American).
 2. Mathematical/Prognostic tools (Renke & Letkiewicz model).
 3. Theoretical frameworks ("Holi-system" model in Appendix).
- **Fix:** Be specific. Use "System organizacyjny" for the French/German comparison, and "Model prognostyczny" for the Renke data to avoid confusion.

**Issue 3: Transition Abruptness**
- **Location:** Section 2.1 (End) → Section 2.2 (Start)
- **Problem:** Section 2.1 ends with a strong conclusion about transformation. Section 2.2 starts with a standard "This chapter presents..." boilerplate.
- **Fix:** Add a bridging sentence at the start of 2.2. E.g., "Given the transformation identified in the literature, a specific methodological approach is required to analyze these changes in the Pomeranian context."

**Issue 4: Citation Granularity**
- **Location:** Section 2.3
- **Problem:** The claim "ponad 10 mln PLN rocznie" is made. While the text mentions the Renke model generally, this specific number needs a direct citation immediately following it to ensure academic integrity.
- **Fix:** Ensure `{cite_XXX}` is placed directly after "10 mln PLN".
